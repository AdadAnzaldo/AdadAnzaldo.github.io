It consists of the design and implementation of a hexapod robot inspired by the locomotion of a common ant, integrating an advanced fuzzy control system and artificial vision for the detection and analysis of pests in vegetable crops.

Using a bio-inspired gait pattern and stereoscopic vision, it can identify targets and adjust its trajectory precisely, maintaining high stability even on irregular terrain.
